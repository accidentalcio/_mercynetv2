({{ page.date | date: "%B %-d, %Y"}}) This summer (May-August) Di’lishi Frozen Yogurt Bar is spotlighting three local non-profits through their community giveback program. Customers can vote for their favorite non-profit by using a designated voting spoon with every yogurt purchased. Then a portion of the profits are donated to the winners, based on the number of votes received. Head on out to Di’lishi in the Oconee Connector Shopping Center (1791 Oconee Connector 430, Athens, GA 30606) to enjoy some delicious frozen yogurt and to support Mercy Health Center!
