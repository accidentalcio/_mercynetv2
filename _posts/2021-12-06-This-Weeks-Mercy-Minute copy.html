Meet our Volunteers - Matthew Lynch

Matthew said he decided to volunteer at Mercy because he was looking for a way to combine his interests in Healthcare and serving vulnerable populations, and a friend told him about Mercy Health Center. During his two and a half years he has volunteered at Mercy, he has served on the prayer team, the eligibility team, and as a clinic manager. His favorite part has been encouraging patients and volunteers to make the most of their situation and see God's hand in the little things.

We are so grateful for your commitment to our mission and for all you do for our patients, Matthew!

Volunteer Impact

Mercy's Mission to provide quality and whole person healthcare to the underserved in our community is only made possible by our amazing volunteers! This year over 500 Individuals volunteered at Mercy. Our volunteers donated over 31,000 hours of their time with us, with the estimated value of their time being over $540,000.
