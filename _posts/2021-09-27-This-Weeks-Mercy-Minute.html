Mercy Volunteer Spotlight

Meet our incredible volunteer, Suvitha Viswanathan! Suvitha has dedicated over 376 hours of her time to Mercy over the last two and a half years. She has served as a check-in volunteer, liaison, intern, and currently serves as a scribe for our providers. Suvitha is graduating in May of 2022 with a major in Biology and a minor in Human Development and Family Science and Women’s Studies. She plans to attend medical school after graduating from the University of Georgia. She wants to focus on OB/GYN studies. Thank you Suvitha for all your hard work and dedication to Mercy!
