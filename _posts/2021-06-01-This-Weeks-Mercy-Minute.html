Volunteers Needed for UGA COVID-19 Study

Researchers at UGA are conducting a research study to learn more about COVID-19 infection and immunity, and this study will be taking place at Mercy Health Center. We are looking for adults who spend at least one day/week in an environment that places them at higher risk for COVID-19. Blood draws and saliva samples are required, but volunteers will be awarded $30 each time they participate.

For information regarding the study visit: http://projectsparta.org or contact sparta-study@uga.edu or (706)-583-0174
