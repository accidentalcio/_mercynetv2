Meet the Team - Karina Lloyd

Karina serves as Mercy’s Intake and Eligibility Specialist. She meets with new patients everyday and makes sure they are qualified for our services. Her job includes fact checking, record keeping, and advocacy on behalf of our patients. She also helps create patient charts and makes appropriate patient referrals for all of our services.

When talking with patients she collects medical, financial and personal information. She even discusses spirituality with our patients.

Karina says she has been blessed to work with such hardworking and awesome coworkers for the past 7 years!

We are so thankful for Karina’s dedication to our mission. Her job is so important because without patients, we have no one to serve.
