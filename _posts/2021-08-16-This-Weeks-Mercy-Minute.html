Mercy Health Center 20th Anniversary

Starting today, August 16th, Mercy is kicking off its 20th Anniversary Campaign to raise $40,000 for necessary lab testing to help patients receive a timely diagnosis and treatment plan. Give today, you will save a life!

For this campaign we are asking you, as a faithful friend of Mercy, to invest in our mission and a desire to eliminate a barrier to care in a Christ centered loving environment.

What we are asking:

  • Donate directly to Mercy's 20th anniversary campaign
  • Share the good news about Mercy's 20th anniversary with friends, family and colleagues.

We greatly appreciate your gifts and prayers!
