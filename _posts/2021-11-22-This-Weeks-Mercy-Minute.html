Mercy Gives Thanks!

We wanted to thank everyone who signed up for our Open House event last Wednesday and for all of those who came out and attended! Our staff members and volunteers were proud to show everyone the 'Heart and Soul' of Mercy. We look forward to hosting more tours of Mercy in the upcoming year!

Meet the Team

This week we wanted to shout out our amazing Pharmacy Staff, MMAPP Coordinator, and Volunteer Coordinator!

Our Pharmacy staff Includes Susie Liu, Angela Miller, Missy Sennowitz, and Tracy Trigleth. These ladies work hard to make sure our patients prescriptions are filled Monday through Friday!

Our MMAP Coordinator is Mandy Baxley. Mandy works with our patients who need more expensive medications like insulin and inhalers. She meets with patients and helps to guide them through the process of receiving their medications directly from pharmaceutical companies.

Our Volunteer Coordinator is Stephanie Woelfl. Stephanie works with our many student, physician, and individual volunteers who want to spend their generous time at Mercy.

We are so thankful for these amazing staff members and all of the hard work they do for Mercy!

Corporate Sponsor Highlight

Thank you to The Banyan Tree for your ongoing partnership in our mission to provide quality and whole-person care for our underserved neighbors. The Banyan Tree has given generously to Mercy's mission and we are deeply grateful for their continued support.
