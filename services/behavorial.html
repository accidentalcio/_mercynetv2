Behavioral Health Process

Commonly Treated Conditions at Mercy Health Center include, but are not limited to:
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Grief and Loss
  • Trauma
  • Interpersonal relationship concerns
  • Mind and body wellness
  • Stress management
  • Sleep Hygiene
  • Adjusting to transitions across the lifecycle
Services Offered for all patients established with Mercy Health Center
  • Integrated Behavioral Health in Primary Care Visits for all New Patient Appointments
  • Assessment (contact our clinic to learn more details about assessments offered)
  • Individual, culturally sensitive counseling available in both English and Spanish
  • Topic specific groups
  • Collaborative Behavioral Health medication management with Primary Care
  • Assistance with outside referrals when necessary

**Mercy Health Center does not provide 24-hour crisis management or inpatient services**
