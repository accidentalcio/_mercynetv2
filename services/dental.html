Dental Services at Mercy

Mercy offers dental services to established patients such as dental cleanings, extractions, and fillings.Mercy does not provide emergency dental care.

To schedule a dental appointment, established patients must call the dental line on the 1st Wednesday of the month starting at 1pm. The dental line number is 706-425-4044. Appointments are scheduled on a first come first serve basis until all appointments are filled.

You must be a Mercy medical patient before you can become a Mercy dental patient.
