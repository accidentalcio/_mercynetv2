Social Services

We have 2 staff Social Workers, as well as MSW Interns and a team of Social Work volunteers available to help you any time Mercy is open. Call our office to set up an appointment, or ask to speak with a social worker the next time you are in the clinic. See below for some other external social services.

Transportation

To get to Mercy Health Center you can take Athens Transit, route #7 - Prince Avenue. A link to information about this route can be found here. Mercy is located right next to Bishop Park off Oglethorpe Avenue.

Medications

For medications not serviced by Mercy's pharmacy, here are some resources for getting your medications at a discounted price or free.

GoodRx is a prescription discount service. GoodRx works similarly to a coupon at the grocery store. Use this card at all pharmacies for a discount on your prescriptions. Get a discount card for your medicine here or pickup a GoodRx card in our office.

Publix Pharmacies offer a number of medications for free. To see a list of medicaitons they offer for free, click here . They also offer some medications for a discounted price. Check here .
