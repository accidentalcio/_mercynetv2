Get Involved at Mercy

Pray

First and foremost, we ask that you pray for Mercy Health Center and its continued growth and development. The needs are great, and the opportunities for service are endless. In the face of these many needs, we want to remain true to God’s will for this ministry.

Meals for Mercy

On nights with extended clinic hours, many volunteers come straight from school or work to serve Mercy’s patients. One way to be a part of the work at Mercy is to help provide a meal on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday evening (5:30pm) for approximately 35 volunteers. We strongly believe that happy volunteers, who are not hungry, make happy patients! If you are interested in feeding volunteers, please contact Angie Johnson by phone: 706-425-9445 ext. 202 or by email at: angie@mercyhealthcenter.net.

Donate Supplies

You can help us reduce operating costs by donating needed medical, dental, pharmaceutical, and office supplies and equipment or in-kind services (i.e. printing, graphic design, maintenance & electrical services). For starters, check out our list of a few needed items below, and contact us for more information about specific needs.

Wish List
  • Copy Paper
  • Hand Soap
  • Travel size toiletries
  • White Cardstock
  • Forever Postage Stamps
  • Mailing Labels
  • Toilet Paper
  • Paper Towels
  • Lysol Wipes
  • Smoking Cessation Supplies (patches, gum, lozenges)
  • Medium Exam Gloves – Latex free

Items can be sent directly to Mercy Health Center or brought to our location at 700 Oglethorpe Ave. Suite C7, Athens, GA 30606. For more details about donating items, please contact: 706-425-9445 or by email: info@mercyhealthcenter.net.

Contribute

As a 501© (3) nonprofit organization, we rely on financial contributions of individuals, foundations, churches, and businesses to support our work. All donations are tax-deductible. You can reach us via email at info@mercyhealthcenter.net or by calling (706) 425-9445 ext. 204 if you are interested in contributing on an ongoing basis or if you would like more information.
