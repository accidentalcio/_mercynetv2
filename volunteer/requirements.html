Patient Support Volunteer

Thank you for your interest in serving our patients at Mercy Health Center. Applications for Spring 2022 semester will be accepted until November 19.

Mercy Health Center is volunteer-supported. You do not have to be a healthcare professional to join us in this effort. We have many volunteer opportunities that extend beyond those of providing patient care during clinic hours. Currently, our volunteers serve in these and other areas:

  • Administrative Assistance
  • Medical, Pharmacy and Dental Care
  • Emotional and Spiritual Care
  • Fundraising and Marketing
  • Building Maintenance

Your application will be reviewed, and you will be contacted within 2-4 weeks IF your availability and skill set align with our team needs for Spring Semester. If you do not receive an email, you may reapply for the Summer Semester in March 2022.

To submit your application for Spring Semester 2022, please complete the attached Google Form. https://forms.gle/jXGg2qpRcBmvsU6T6

If you are interested in volunteering as a MD, DMD, PA, NP, PharmD, PHD, RN, or LCSW, etc., please see the Clinical Volunteers page.
