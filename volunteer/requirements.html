Patient Support Volunteer

Mercy Health Center is volunteer-supported. You do not have to be a healthcare professional to join us in this effort. We have many volunteer opportunities that extend beyond those of providing patient care during clinic hours. Currently, our volunteers serve in these and other areas:

  • Administrative Assistance
  • Medical, Pharmacy and Dental Care
  • Emotional and Spiritual Care
  • Fundraising and Marketing
  • Building Maintenance

Applications for the FALL semester will be accepted June 21 - July 2. To ensure that your application is reviewed and properly processed, please complete and submit ALL forms as a SINGLE PDF with the subject line NEW VOLUNTEER APPLICATION to volunteer@mercyhealthcenter.net:

  • Patient Support Application
  • Current Semester Schedule
  • Copy of Immunization Record
  • Copy of TB Skin Test (within last 3 months)
  • Background Check (obtain from local police or sheriffâ€™s department)
